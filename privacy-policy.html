Privacy Policy

At Old Brooklyn Computer Company, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers' personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you engage with our services, we may collect personal information such as your name, phone number, and email address.

    •  

  • Device Information: When you bring a device in for repair, we may collect information related to the device, such as model number, serial number, and any issues you report.

    •  

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide Services: We use your personal information to process orders, repair your devices, and deliver the services you request.

    •  

  • To Communicate with You: We may use your contact information to send you updates about your repair and respond to your inquiries.

    •  

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

At Old Brooklyn Computer Company, we are committed to your privacy and take it very seriously. We do not sell, share, or give your personal information to anyone, under any circumstances, period. Your data remains strictly confidential and is used solely to provide the services you request.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy. These updates are designed to ensure that our policy remains current and accurately reflects how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly so that you remain informed about how we are safeguarding your privacy and any new measures we have implemented to enhance the security of your data.
